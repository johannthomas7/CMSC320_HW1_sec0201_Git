
Q1: Why might we consider usign Git?

Answer: It makes it convenient to share code when working on projects as a
group.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to almost any field as it is always
important to be able to look at information and be able to draw conclusions
from it. For example in healthcare, you may want to analyze and visualize
data relating to certain diseases like how many people may be affected within
a certain population in order to help you make decisions on what diseases to
try to address as a priority.

Q3: What is the differnece between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the history of the two branches, whereas rebasing
rewrites it, making the history of the project linear instead of one where
there is a joining of two distinct branches.
